About Us

Welcome to Sri VR Chary Restaurants & Tiffins! We started our journey with a passion for providing fresh, delicious, and healthy meals to our customers. Our goal is to bring happiness to every plate and make every meal a memorable experience.

Founded by Arendula Mohan Chary, our restaurant focuses on serving traditional flavors with a modern touch. From freshly prepared tiffins to full-course meals, we ensure quality, hygiene, and taste in every dish.

We also offer convenient online orders and timely delivery, so you can enjoy your favorite meals wherever you are.
